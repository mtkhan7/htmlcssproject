About Me

My name is Mo, I am an aspiring software developer and a huge avid fan of the World Of Warcraft game. Since an early age, I have had a great interest within technology and a passion to pursue a career in software development.

I am fascinated by how technology is now a vital part in our lives and is constantly changing. I have experience of working in multiple positions such as providing technical support and customer service. Currently, I feel I am at a level in my career where I would like to further develop and challenge myself to learn software development. I feel that to enhance one's knowledge, a change of environment is fundamental in doing so therefore taking the opportunity to learn software development at Code Nation was ideal.

My experience at Code Nation has been highly productive. I have gained an insight in many areas such as best practices in coding and development. I have actively applied my gained knowledge in coding and project management into practice through completing tasks such as developing a game in collaboration with other colleagues as part of a team. Throughout my time I have learned about what is expected within the I.T sector and opportunities available to those pursuing this sector.
